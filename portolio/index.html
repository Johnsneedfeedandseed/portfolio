About Me

Joaquin Arriola (handle @Johnsneedart) is an illustrator with over three years of focused practice in observational and anatomical drawing. He works primarily in grayscale, translating complex forms into clear, expressive studies for editorial, portrait, and commission-based projects.

3+ Years of practice
80+ Commissions
60+ Returning clients

Joaquin combines traditional pen-and-ink techniques with careful value studies to create work that is both precise and emotionally resonant. He approaches each commission with a collaborative mindset, offering clear timelines, progress sketches, and responsive revisions to ensure the final piece meets editorial or client needs.

My Work

I specialize in several areas of illustration:

Editorial

Illustrations for magazines, books and digital publications that capture the essence of each story.

Branding

Unique visual identities that help brands connect emotionally with their audience.

Digital

Concept art and character design for games, animation and interactive media.

Each project is an opportunity to explore new techniques and visual styles.

My Creative Process

This is how I turn ideas into illustrations:

1. Research & Concept

I gather references, create moodboards and define the visual direction for the project.

2. Sketching & Exploration

I produce quick gesture sketches and thumbnails followed by a refined sketch for client review.

3. Refinement & Delivery

I finalize ink and grayscale rendering, digitize at production resolution, apply revisions and deliver web/print-ready files.
